About us

Our company was founded in 2010 by an entrepreneur-engineer and his several friends, who are passionate about inventing. The main reason for setting up Small Innovations is that we wanted to be independent and apply our creativity and skills in real projects.

We support our clients when they need new ideas for their business, want to reduce the complexity of a product or find an elegant technical solution.

Our family-like team consists of 9 enthusiastic people who are passionate about inventing. Our main value is to build a reliable communication with our client to handle their project in the best way

Our technical knowledge, equipment and creativity are at your service. We always work for the result to make people satisfied!
